Hotdog

A hotdog is a popular fast food consisting of a cooked sausage, usually made of beef, pork, or a combination, served in a sliced bun. It is often topped with various condiments such as ketchup, mustard, onions, relish, or sauerkraut.


Ingredients

  • Sausages (beef, pork, or chicken)
  • Hotdog buns
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Onions
  • Relish
  • Sauerkraut

Steps on how to make Hotdog

  1. Heat a skillet or grill over medium heat.
  2. Cook sausages until they are browned and heated through.
  3. Toast hotdog buns on the skillet or grill until lightly browned.
  4. Place cooked sausages in the toasted buns.
  5. Add desired condiments such as ketchup, mustard, onions, relish, or sauerkraut on top of the sausages.
