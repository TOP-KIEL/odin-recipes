Bacon

Bacon is a cured meat product made from pork belly, typically sliced thin and either pan-fried or baked until crispy. Known for its savory, smoky flavor and crispy texture, bacon is a popular ingredient in various dishes or enjoyed on its own as a breakfast staple.

Ingredients

  • Bacon-wrapped Dates
  • Bacon and Egg Breakfast Sandwich
  • Bacon-wrapped Asparagus
  • Bacon Mac and Cheese
  • Bacon-wrapped Chicken Tenders

Steps on how to make bacon

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Arrange Bacon: Place bacon slices on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper or aluminum foil, ensuring they don't overlap.
  3. Bake: Place the baking sheet in the preheated oven and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the bacon is crispy and golden brown.
  4. Drain Excess Fat: Carefully remove the bacon from the baking sheet and transfer it to a plate lined with paper towels to drain excess fat.
  5. Serve: Once drained, serve the bacon hot and enjoy its crispy deliciousness.
